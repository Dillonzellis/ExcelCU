Why Get a Personal Loan?

So why take out a personal loan? There are many reasons individuals may explore this option that depend on personal and financial factors. Loans can differ depending on credit history, credit score, payment history, and other personal financial factors.

Some popular reasons people take out credit union personal loans include:

  • Moving / Home Costs: If you’re gearing up for a home remodel or a big move, a personal loan can help ease extra costs during this transition.
  • Vehicles: Personal loans can be instrumental in covering costs for vehicles like cars, boats, and RVs.
  • Emergencies: Emergencies strike at random, and sometimes you do not have the funds to cover a repair or accident. Some people use personal loans to fix cars, home appliances, or medical emergencies.
  • Large Purchases: If you are gearing up to make a large purchase, like a new heating system or washer, personal loans can help cover the upfront cost

How Can I Use a Personal Loan?

Personal loans can be used however the loaner wants them to be, depending on your financial situation. There are some restrictions on personal loans to make them a viable option for loaners that wish to pay off debt or achieve other personal financial goals. Loans can also vary depending on credit score or lines of credit that have been opened.

Some of the common ways loaners use their funds include:
